The House

A stone home,
above the Aegean

Androikies Stone is a traditional two-storey stone house nestled on the hillside above Agios Petros beach, just outside Gavrio. With three bedrooms, two bathrooms and a generous terrace framing uninterrupted sea views, it offers everything you need to slow down and settle into island life.

The house is faithful to the architecture of Andros — thick stone walls that stay cool in summer, arched doorways, and raw natural textures — updated with the comfort and light you want on holiday.

Getting Here

How to reach
Androikies

Andros is one of the closest Cycladic islands to Athens — easy to reach by ferry from Rafina port, with several daily departures in summer.

From Athens
Drive or take a bus to Rafina port (~30 min from Athens city centre, ~45 min from the airport).

Ferries to Gavrio run several times daily. Journey time is approximately 2 hours by fast ferry or 2.5 hours by conventional ferry.

Book tickets at ferryscanner.com or bluestarferries.com.
By Air
Andros has no airport. Fly into Athens International Airport (ATH), then head to Rafina port for the ferry.

Rafina is the closest port to the airport — only 20–25 minutes by taxi or the Rafina–Airport bus.

This makes it one of the easiest island connections from any international flight.
From Gavrio Port to the House
Once you dock at Gavrio, Androikies Stone is just 1.5 km away — a 3-minute drive towards Agios Petros beach.

Taxis are available at the port. Renting a car is recommended for exploring the island — there are rental offices in Gavrio and Batsi.
